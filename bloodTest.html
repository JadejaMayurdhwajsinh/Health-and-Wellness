Blood Test

The Blood Test Checker is a simple tool that analyzes hemoglobin levels based on the user's age. Users input their full name, age, and hemoglobin level, and the checker compares the provided values against standard age-specific healthy hemoglobin ranges. Depending on the results, it displays a message indicating whether the hemoglobin level is normal or below the recommended range, suggesting further medical consultation if necessary.
